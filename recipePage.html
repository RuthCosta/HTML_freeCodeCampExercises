Cake Recipe

This is the recipe for a delicious cake

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Yeast
  • Wheat Flour

Instructions

  1. Put the eggs in a bowl and mix it with the milk and the sugar.
  2. Now add the wheat flour.
  3. And last put the yeast slowly on the recipe.
  4. Then put it in a cake tin to bake.
  5. Bake it in an oven preheated.
